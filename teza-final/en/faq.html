FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the questions we get asked most. Can't find yours? Write to us — we're happy to help.

How much does it cost? How is pricing determined?
Pricing depends on the solution you need. Since every project is custom-built, we set pricing after an initial call that lets us fully understand your needs and the scope of the work.
How long does it take to set up an automation?
Timelines depend on the solution and its complexity. We give you a clear estimate at the start of the project, once your needs are well defined.
Do I have to change my current tools?
Our goal is not to disrupt your habits: we work with the tools you already use. A change is sometimes necessary, but we always explain it clearly and keep it to a strict minimum.
Does it work with the Google environment?
Yes. Our solutions integrate with the Google environment (Calendar, Gmail, Drive and more), as well as many other platforms.
What happens if something breaks, or after delivery?
We handle monitoring and bug tracking, with guarantees on the work delivered. You're not left on your own once the solution ships: we stay present to ensure it keeps running smoothly.
Is my data secure?
The security of your data is an absolute priority. We adapt to each company's needs, including, for example, hosting your databases in Canada if you wish.
Do you work with businesses of my size or in my industry?
We work with all types of businesses, from SMBs to large companies, across all industries. The one thing our clients have in common: they want to save time.
