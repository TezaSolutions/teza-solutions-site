We're launching MARK Copilot, our first conversational agent

After months building and testing our own automation systems internally, we're ready to make our first official solution available: MARK Copilot.

MARK Copilot is a conversational agent designed to handle one of the most repetitive — and time-consuming — tasks in running a business: booking appointments and managing calendars.

What MARK Copilot actually does

Instead of leaving your customers waiting for a reply, MARK Copilot talks to them directly, checks your availability and confirms the appointment.

  • Conversational appointment booking
  • Calendar management and sync
  • Automatic reminders to reduce no-shows
  • A clear dashboard to track everything
The goal isn't to replace human contact, but to remove everything that used to eat up valuable time before reaching it.

Why now

This solution came from a very real need, first tested internally to manage several businesses at once. Once the system was stable and reliable, it became obvious it could serve other entrepreneurs facing the same headache.
