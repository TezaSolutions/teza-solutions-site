Frequently asked questions

Answers to the questions we get asked most

How much does it cost? How is pricing determined?
Pricing depends on the solution you need. Since every project is custom-built, we set pricing after an initial call that lets us fully understand your needs and the scope of the work.
Do I have to change my current tools?
Our goal is not to disrupt your habits: we work with the tools you already use. A change is sometimes necessary, but we always explain it clearly and keep it to a strict minimum.
Is my data secure?
The security of your data is an absolute priority. We adapt to each company's needs, including, for example, hosting your databases in Canada if you wish.
